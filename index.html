HIDE THE GREEN

How to play :

  • Choose a pattern.
  • Choose three blocks on the board where you want to place the pattern.
  • To place the pattern click on the block of the board corresponding to the middle block of the pattern.
  • Repeat these steps until there is any further move possible.
  • If you succeed in covering all the green blocks using minimum number of pattern then you won the game.
  • Otherwise you loose the game.
  • To start the game click on the "Start Game" button

Best of luck!
